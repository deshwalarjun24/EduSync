Exam Regulations

Student ID Requirements

All students must bring their college ID card to the examination hall. No entry will be permitted without proper identification.

Timing & Punctuality

Students should arrive at least 15 minutes before the exam starts. No entry will be allowed after 30 minutes from the start of the exam.

Prohibited Items

Mobile phones, smart watches, calculators (unless specified), and any electronic devices are strictly prohibited in the examination hall.

Malpractice Consequences

Any form of malpractice will result in disqualification from the exam and may lead to severe disciplinary action as per college norms.
